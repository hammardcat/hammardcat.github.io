About HammerDCat

HammerDCat was born from a revolutionary idea: what if stuffed cats were flat? What began as an accident in our founder's garage (when a hammer was accidentally dropped on a stuffed cat) has grown into a beloved brand known for its perfectly flattened stuffed cats.

Each of our flattened cats is precisely bonked with our proprietary HammerMatic 3000™ technology. We use only the finest, most compressible materials to ensure that our cats are not only adorably flat but also durable and safe.

Our team of skilled flatteners takes pride in creating perfectly compressed cats that capture the unique charm of a cat that has just had a cartoon anvil dropped on it. Whether you're looking for a classic tabby pancake, an elegant Siamese flatbread, or a custom-designed cat pressed to your exact specifications, we can bring your flattened vision to life.

At HammerDCat, we believe in creating more than just flat toys – we create compressed companions that slide perfectly under doors, fit in envelopes, and become cherished members of your family.

How We Process Your Cat

Witness our revolutionary cat flattening process that transforms ordinary cats into perfectly compressed stuffed companions.

The BONK™ Process: From Fluffy to Flat in Seconds!

1

Selection

We carefully select premium cat shapes and materials that are perfect for the flattening process. Only the best cats are chosen for hammering.

2

Preparation

Cats are prepared for flattening by removing any rigid components that might interfere with the perfect compression process.

3

The Bonk

This is where the magic happens! Our master flatteners use our proprietary HammerMatic 3000™ to deliver the perfect bonk, compressing the cat to ideal flatness.

4

Compression Check

Each cat is carefully measured to ensure optimal flatness. If not flat enough, it goes back for additional bonking until perfection is achieved.

5

Recovery Period

Flattened cats need time to adjust to their new dimensions. We let them rest in our specially designed compression chambers for 24 hours.

6

Final Inspection

Every flattened cat undergoes a thorough inspection to ensure it meets our high standards of flatness, durability, and aesthetic appeal before being shipped to you.

Why CatDrone?

Our engineers asked a simple question: "What's better than a flattened cat?" The answer was obvious - a flattened cat that can fly!

The CatDrone Conversion System uses our already perfectly flattened cats as the ideal platform for drone technology. Their aerodynamic shape and lightweight construction make them perfect for flight.

Each CatDrone is equipped with a quadcopter propulsion system, HD camera, and remote control capabilities. Perfect for aerial photography, surveillance, or just impressing your neighbors!
